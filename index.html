Local Crate

The big thing! This is the capstone project for my whole experience as a student at Prime Digital Academy. Local Crate asked us to create a space where their vendors, farmers, makers and suppliers could share information or the "what's up" in their world to the Local Crate customers. A group of four of us tackled this project and we couldn't be happier with what we created for them in 3 weeks.

therMobile

TherMobile is a mobile-friendly full-stack web application to track Internet of Things (IoT) devices. TherMobile allows a user to connect an IoT temperature logging device to their dashboard for logging of device data to a database and control real world things based on this data. This JavaScript app is built in React.js and originated as my solo project at Prime Digital Academy. It persists as a limitless platform for continued learning, tinkering and building.
